Models Parrot Medchem Concepts but Cannot Predict Structural Effects

A controlled benchmark measuring whether LLMs can reason about ADME property changes from molecular modifications -- or just pattern-match medchem vocabulary.

Headline Finding

The "Chess" Test: Seeing Ahead vs. Being Told

Strategic planning tasks ask models to choose multi-step optimization paths. When the effects of step 2 are shown, the task reduces to arithmetic. When they are hidden, models must actually predict structural effects.

Unexpected Finding

Sonnet Outperforms Opus on the Hardest Tasks

On the two hardest task categories, Sonnet leads Opus by a consistent margin. The direction is the same for both task types.

0
Sonnet advantage on transform_ranking
0
Sonnet advantage on strategic_planning
Hypothesis: Opus may overthink -- producing longer, more hedged analyses that paradoxically lead to worse final selections. Sonnet's more direct pattern matching appears better calibrated for ranking structural transforms by ADME impact. The overall gap is 90.6 vs 85.9 (Sonnet leads by 4.7 points).
Caveat: Sample size is n=4 per task type. The direction is consistent across both hard task types, but larger-scale runs are needed to confirm the magnitude.
Reference

What Each Task Type Tests

multi_objective_path
Choose a molecular modification path that optimizes multiple ADME endpoints simultaneously, balancing competing objectives.
sacrifice_detection
Identify which ADME property is being sacrificed in a structural change that improves other properties -- a tradeoff awareness test.
transform_explain
Explain why a given structural transformation has its observed effect on a specific ADME endpoint -- mechanistic reasoning.
series_completion
Given a congeneric series of molecules with measured properties, predict the ADME profile of a held-out analog.
tradeoff_analysis
Analyze the multi-endpoint consequences of a structural transform -- which properties improve, which degrade, and by how much.
property_delta
Predict the direction and magnitude of property change from a specific structural modification, with mechanistic explanation.
strategic_planning
Plan a multi-step optimization sequence, avoiding greedy local choices that preclude better long-term outcomes.
transform_ranking
Rank a set of structural transforms by their expected effect on an ADME endpoint -- requires quantitative intuition about transform impact.
